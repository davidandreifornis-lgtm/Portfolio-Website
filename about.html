About Me

Junior Data Analyst, in progress

Hi, I'm David — I turn raw, messy data into information people can actually act on. My background is hands-on: real fieldwork, real databases, real reporting deadlines.

I worked as an Enumerator under the Special Program for Employment of Students (SPES) at Calamba City Hall, where I collect, verify, and manage data across multiple city programs. Alongside that, I'm finishing my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at City College of Calamba.

I care about accuracy first — a clean, validated dataset beats a flashy dashboard built on bad numbers. Whether I'm gathering data in the field, structuring a database, or building a digital system from scratch, the goal is the same: information people can trust enough to act on.
